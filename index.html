FEATURED STORY

Meet Zaffar Khan

Zaffar Khan exemplifies the strength of the human spirit. A dedicated community leader, advocate for people with disabilities, and active member of the Rotary Club, Zaffar has spent his life in service to others.

When faced with unprecedented challenges, he demonstrated that true leadership means rising above circumstances and continuing to serve with grace, dignity, and unwavering commitment. His story is one of transformation — showing how personal trials can become sources of strength and inspiration for others.

FEATURED PROFILE

The Film maker — Eric Kagorora

IN HIS OWN WORDS

E ric Kagorora is more than a film maker—he is a storyteller, a cultural bridge, and a passionate advocate for healing through art. As the founder of Imanzi Cultural Collective Australia, Eric brings a unique blend of lived experience and creative vision to UNBROKEN.

“Film is not just a medium for me—it’s a way to give voice to the unheard, to transform pain into purpose, and to inspire hope where it’s needed most.”

Through trauma-informed storytelling and a deep commitment to cultural healing, Eric uses the power of film to illuminate resilience and foster lasting change. His work is a testament to the strength found in community, the importance of representation, and the enduring impact of sharing our stories.
